About Me

I’m Ruchi Bajracharya, a Computer Science student and an aspiring front-end developer passionate about building user-friendly web and mobile applications. I have keen interest in work with technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Flutter. A fast learner with a detail-oriented approach, I believe in solving problems with creative and efficient solutions while staying committed to deadlines.

In addition to coding, I bring my passion for badminton and dancing into my life as a way to stay active, energetic and balanced. I aspire to work on impactful projects that combine technical innovation with seamless user experiences. Let’s connect to discuss how I can contribute to your team with my technical expertise and innovative mindset!
